I am a Professor of Political Science at Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria, and currently serve as Head of Department. With a PhD in Political Science and extensive international experience, I specialize in governance, public policy, electoral processes, conflict prevention, and development studies.

My consulting work spans across Africa and beyond, working with prestigious organizations including the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), UNICEF, African Union, UNODC, European Union, and the Council for the Development of Social Sciences Research in Africa (CODESRIA).

I have held visiting professorships and research fellowships at renowned institutions including the University of Johannesburg, University of Gothenburg (Sweden), McGill University (Canada), and the University of Zambia.
